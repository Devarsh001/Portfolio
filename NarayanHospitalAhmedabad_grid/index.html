Overview

Narayana Multispeciality Hospital Ahmedabad is a tertiary care hospital offers medical services for Paediatric and Adult Cardiac, Neurology, Orthopaedics, Nephrology & Urology, Obstetrics & Gynecology, General Medicine, Gastroenterology, ENT, Paediatrics, Pulmonology services. It is among the leading hospitals of Gujrat to provide all round cancer care. A fully equipped cardiac sciences department has a Digital Cath Lab, Coronary Care Unit (CCU) and Intensive Therapy Unit (ITU). Neurosciences department offers Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery and Microsurgery for conditions like disc prolapse, spine fractures, spinal tumours and similar ailments other than treatment of epilepsy. The key Orthopaedics procedures include replacement surgeries for knee, hip and shoulder. NH Ahmedabad is an established and reliable healthcare services provider for patients from Gujarat & many other international locations.
